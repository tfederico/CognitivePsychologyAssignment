Online dementia test

In this website, you will find a serie of test. Please complete them on your own without any kind of help. Nobody, except for the doctor, will know that you have done this test, so feel free about your answers. If you have already done a test in this website, please log in before starting a new test. If this is your first time, please subscribe in order to keep track of your progress. You will find both of these options in the upper right corner of this page. In case you have any doubt, you can click on the red dot mark in the lower right corner of this page. A menu with different options will appear.
